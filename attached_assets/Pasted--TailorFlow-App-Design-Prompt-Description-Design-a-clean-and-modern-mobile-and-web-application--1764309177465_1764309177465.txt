# TailorFlow App Design Prompt

## Description
Design a clean and modern mobile and web application interface for TailorFlow, a customer and employee management platform tailored for small tailoring shops and boutique businesses. Include a well-crafted home page with a refined, minimalistic logo that reflects precision, creativity, and craftsmanship.

## Style
Modern, elegant, and intuitive user experience using:
- Neutral color tones
- Clear typography
- Simple navigation
- Smooth interactions
- Intuitive icons

---

## Core Pages and Features

### 1. Home / Dashboard
- Display quick stats: total active orders, completed work, today’s revenue, pending payments.
- Include quick links to Customers, Employees, Orders, Payments, Inventory, and Analytics.
- Prominently show the logo and brand name “TailorFlow” at the top.

### 2. Customer Management
- Customer profile cards with contact details, order history, and measurement data.
- Tabs for personal info, measurements, images (fabric, completed designs), and notes.
- Option to send notifications or messages (SMS/app).

### 3. Order Management
- Create, edit, and track orders from consultation to delivery.
- Status flow: Pending → In Progress → Completed → Delivered.
- Attach order images and notes.
- Search and filter by customer name or order ID.

### 4. Employee Management
- Employee profile with contact info, assigned tasks, attendance, and payroll summary.
- Task assignment interface to allocate tailoring jobs with due dates.
- Performance metrics dashboard to track output and quality.

### 5. Payment Management
- Generate digital invoices with itemized charges (fabric, tailoring, alterations).
- Support multiple payment modes: cash, card, UPI, wallet, bank transfer.
- Manage partial payments, issue receipts, and track outstanding balances.
- Financial summaries with daily/weekly/monthly revenue charts.

### 6. Analytics & Reports
- Dashboard for revenue, orders, and outstanding dues.
- Filters for customers, employees, and payment history.
- Fabric and accessory inventory alerts with reorder suggestions.
- Role-based access control (Admin, Tailor, Manager).
- Mobile-responsive layout for smartphones and tablets.

---

## Branding and Aesthetic
- **Logo Concept:** Thread loop forming a minimalist "T" inside a circle, symbolizing flow and precision.
- **Color Palette:** Soft gold or maroon with white and light gray backgrounds.
- **Font Suggestion:** Modern sans-serif such as Poppins or Lato.
